About Us

Travel Guides

Our travel guide is compiled by the most adventurous of all locals. Our goal is to make it easy for you to see everything Cebu has to offer—the good food, the incredible sights, and everything in between.

Latest Media

We help you plan your trip from start to finish. We have compiled latest videos on what other tourists has to say about Cebu, hotels and restaurants reviews, the latest news on upcoming events and attractions that may be of interest to you.

Book a flight

If you're ready to go, our website has a booking page where you can directly book a flight. We are the one stop shop for all your travel needs in Cebu.

Itinerary

Our mission is to provide locals with fun, stress-free travel ideas in their very own city. With our sample itineraries, we'll help you get out there and go on an adventure without having to leave the Philippines!

With us, you don't have to worry about wasting your precious vacation hours planning and researching the best places to go—we'll do that for you! Just tell us how much time you have and what kind of things you like to do, and we'll create an itinerary customized just for you.
