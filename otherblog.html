Juan Dela Cruz, Mar 5, 2022

Let me tell you a story. It all started with a girl who loves to travel and has a knack for organizing things. She had this idea that she wanted to organize everything about the Queen City and make it accessible for everyone who wants to visit here. She started with her friends, then with her family, then with her acquaintances, and now she's sharing it to you!

She loves making lists of the best places in town and telling stories about them. She loves taking photos and writing reviews. She loves meeting locals and making friends. And most importantly, she loves writing guides that can help others explore the neighborhood.

Cebu is a melting pot of culture and heritage, which is best embodied in its rich gastronomic tradition. Cebuanos are known for their warmth and hospitality and nowhere is it more evident than in their rich food culture. Cebuanos have developed an appreciation for good food. It is also not surprising that they have developed their own unique dishes that have become their pride.
